Job Responsibilities

Each summer the Jaypee University employs 3-4 individuals to serve as Resident/Teaching Assistants for the program of your choice. R/TA’s live on campus in the residence hall with the participating students while assisting the Program Director, Resident Director, and Faculty with all aspects of Residential, Curricular, and Production activities within the program. Primary responsibilities of the Resident/Teaching Assistants include:

  • To actively support the goals and purpose of the college Program and facilitate the engagement of all students in that purpose.
  • To attend and actively participate in all scheduled meetings, training, field trips, and events of the college Program.
  • To maintain the security and cleanliness of all school facilities and property, including student rooms during and at the completion of the program.
  • To refrain from any activities prohibited of college students while in their presence. If an R.A. is of legal age, alcohol is to be consumed off campus. Smoking should occur out of the sight/awareness of the college students and should follow the campus rules regarding designated areas where smoking is banned.
  • Your availability between June 21st and July 12th.
  • To maintain appropriate supervision over all Governor’s School students throughout the duration of the program and to respond in an appropriate and timely manner to student needs, problems, safety, or security concerns.
  • Describe why you would like to serve as an R/TA for our summer program AND what special skills, abilities, talents, etc. that you would bring to serve the program.
