  • Prospective Students

    Jaypee’s focus on interdisciplinary studies allows students to tailor their academic experiences to their career and life goals. As one of the largest public research institutions in the country, The University has thousands of exciting projects underway that encourage strong partnerships between students and faculty.



    Undergraduate

    Jaypee Univeristy’s undergraduate students pursue more than 90 majors across four schools and engage in interdisciplinary programs that make the resources of all 12 schools available as they forge new paths in research and scholarship.
