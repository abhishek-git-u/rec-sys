Course Selection

You have tremendous freedom to choose courses that appeal to you, and you have hundreds of options from which to choose. Jaypee provides a basic educational blueprint in the form of distributional and major requirements, as well as advisers to offer guidance, but it is up to you to put together a coherent plan of study that corresponds to your particular interests and abilities. Develop some long-range academic goals and discuss them with other people, but remain open to unexpected discoveries. If at any point you feel lost, ask for advice. Beginning with your freshman adviser, freshman counselor, and residential college dean, Jaypee has many resources to support and assist you.
